Train Schedules & Timings from Major Cities

The city of Dehradun is well connected from other major cities all over India via Delhi and hence one can easy get here if you are have thinking of coming to this amazing place along with friends and family. Below you will get the train schedules from different locations and plan your trip accordingly as per your plan.

irctc.co.in

TRAINS FROM NEW DELHI TO DEHRADUN

There are majorly three trains that you could catch from New Delhi named Mussoorie, Jan Shatabdi and Shatabdi Express and that depart from there at 9:30 PM, 3:25 PM and 6:50 AM respectively. While their arrival schedule time at the Dehradun junction are respectively 8:00 AM, 9:10 PM and 12:40 PM.

Train Number Train Name Departure Station Departure Time Destination Arrival Time
12055 Jan Shatabdi Express New Delhi 15:25 Dehradun 21:10
14041 Mussoorie Express New Delhi 21:30 Dehradun 8:00
12017 Shatabdi Express New Delhi 6:50 Dehradun 12:40

TRAINS FROM KOLKATA TO DEHRADUN

One can catch up the two trains of Doon and Upasana express from Howrah (Kolkata) to reach Dehradun. These trains depart from the Howrah station at the timings of 8:35 PM and 1:10 PM and reach the destination at 7:10 AM and 6:00 PM respectively.

Train Number Train Name Departure Station Departure Time Destination Arrival Time
13009 Doon Express Howrah (Kolkata), Varanasi, Lucknow 20:35 Dehradun 7:10
12327 Upasana Express Howrah (Kolkata), Lucknow 13:10 Dehradun 18:00

TRAIN FROM MUMBAI TO DEHRADUN

The Mumbai residents can catch up the Bandra express from 11:35 PM at the Bandra terminal to reach the destination at 5:35 PM. This moves through the station of Delhi before coming to Dehradun.

Train Number Train Name Departure Station Departure Time Destination Arrival Time
19019 Bandra Dehradun Express Bandra Terminus (Mumbai), Nizamuddin (Delhi) 23:35 Dehradun 17:35

TRAIN FROM CHENNAI TO DEHRADUN

If you are close to Chennai or are a resident of that place then the best train for you will be the Chennai Express that would depart at 9:45 AM from there and reach at 5:00 PM at its schedule time.

Train Number Train Name Departure Station Departure Time Destination Arrival Time
12687 Chennai Dehradun Express Chennai, Nizamuddin (Delhi) 9:45 Dehradun 5:00

Flight Schedules And Timing

The major and currently the only domestic airport is the Jolly Grant Airport which is located around 25 kms from the city centre. One can catch up a taxi or a cab which would get you right to the airport comfortably. But if its easy for you and are a habitual traveller then the cheaper means to get there is the government buses which move to and fro the city.

Flight Schedule From Delhi To Dehradun

Here you would find below the timings for the two working flights to Dehradun from Delhi: Indigo and Air India.

Flight Number Departure Time Arrival Time Flight Duration
6E-818 15:25 16:15 0h 50m
6E-966 12:35 13:50 1h 15m

There are two flights of Indigo from Delhi to Dehradun, one departing at 15:25 PM and the other at 12:35 PM that arrive at Dehradun airport at 16:15 PM and 13:50 PM respectively.

Flight Number Departure Time Arrival Time Flight Duration
AI-9609 18:10 19:10 1h 00m
AI-9645 07:00 08:05 1h 05m

There are two flights of Air India from Delhi to Dehradun, one departing at 18:10 PM and the other at 07:00 AM that arrive at Dehradun airport at 19:10 PM and 08:05 AM respectively.

Tips for Driving directions to Jolly Grant Airport
  1. From the Dehradun city centre move on to the ISBT terminal to the East with the help of bus or auto or taxi. You could now get the bus or move on with the taxi. Head east on National Highway 72. Pass by Navjyoti Eye Hospital (on the left in 3.4 km).
  2. Turn left onto Haridwar – Dehradun NH72 from Diowala where you would pass by Shed Institute of Paramedical Science & Hospital (on the right in 550 m).
  3. Move on straight from Bhaniyawala without turning to the Haridwar road and you will just reach the Joly Grant Airport to catch your flight.

By Road From Delhi

Sometimes one cannot get the time to book the tickets for train and flight hence have to make your way to the destination all along by road. It could be that one is a travel enthusiast and want to travel by private cars, taxis or buses so as to enjoy the small spots all along the way and hence add to the fun and frolic all the way along with friends and family!

How to reach Dehradun by Bus

One can catch a number of buses that move round the clock for transport of people from one city to the other. There are various state owned government buses that one could catch to reach Dehradun. These may include the various deluxe buses known as Volvo coach if you have the budget. These might be bit costly but the services provided by these luxury buses are really awesome with the kind of cosy seats to sit and lay down for a relaxed journey. So if you have the budget then what could be better than these or otherwise if you have a tight pocket then catch up the interstate normal buses that would take you at a very reasonable pricing to Dehradun.

The ticket price and bus fare will be approximate INR 350 for ordinary buses, INR 600 for semi deluxe and around rupees 850 for the luxury Volvo ones if you are catching these buses from Delhi where you could easily get these from ISBT Kashmiri Gate Bus Station. You might take a travel time of about 6 hrs to reach the Dehradun city during the night but the day travel could be longer depending on the traffic rush and which might be 8 to 9 hrs.

How to reach Dehradun by Taxi or Cab

One can also book the cab ( OLA and UBER ) from Online or can book the cab locally from their locations to get to the amazing tourist place of Dehradun. The route followed by the taxis are almost the same as the local or state buses but it might happen that due to the traffic rush the driver may take you from another road way and via a different direction. This mode of journey gives you the ease of stopping at few places of your choice while on the drive to refresh yourself in hotels all along the road sides or otherwise.

DRIVING DIRECTIONS TO REACH DEHRADUN BY ROAD

  1. Starting from Delhi you would be moving to south east and then to the Deen Dayal Marg. You would then move on to the NH24 via the Indraprastha and Mahatma Gandhi Marg.
  2. After travelling you would then come on to the Merut Bypass and National Highway 58. Continuing along this road you will reach the Saharanpur road SH 59.
  3. Travelling further you will reach the NH 73 from where you could turn to NH 72A which would lead you to the Saharanpur Chowk from where you could get to the Dehradun city centre.
