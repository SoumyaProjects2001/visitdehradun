Pacific Mall

Pacific Mall is Dehradun’s beloved shopping, dining and lifestyle destination. Back in 2013, it was the beginning of this revolutionary project which later evolved into the prime shopping complex in Uttarakhand.The prominent national and international brands joined together to introduce the unmatched shopping experience for the shoppers in Dehradun and neighboring locale. People, from every corner of the city and even beyond, visit this place for fun, entertainment, and obviously for an authentic shopping experience. More than 175 handpicked brands welcome at least 25k shoppers every day.

Opening Hours :- 11:00 AM - 09:00 PM


(Source)

Time square mall

Times square mall, Dehradun, the most awaited shopping destination of the capital city.Times square mall is a visually and thematically boutique designed, all-under-one-roof retail, dining and entertainment destination. It caters to the needs of the entire family and offers on its platter a diverse merchandise mix offering Value, Fashion, Lifestyle and Entertainment options targeting the Upper Middle Income and Middle Income consuming classes.

Opening Hours :- Temporarily Closed


(Source)

Crossroad Mall

Crossroads Mall is a favorite getaway for the young crowd of Dehradun and a shopping destination for young and old alike. Whether you are looking for a place for a family outing or just a hangout location for your group of friends to spend some social time, the Crossroads Mall in Dehradun is a convenient location with many choices of entertainment and shopping. Located in the Old Survey road, the mall is easy to reach and connected to all the means of local transportation.

Opening Hours :- 10:30 AM - 11:00 PM


(Source)

Lavana Mall

Times Square Mall has all popular brands and food outlets, an exquisite game zone and a multiplex, Mukta A2 Cinemas. It is a popular family mall in Dehradun because it offers entertainment as well as shopping options. This mall has many brands such as Reliance Trends, Arrow, Puma.

Opening Hours :- 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM


(Source)

Doon Square Mall

Supertech presents Doon Square; the commercial project that's already the talk of Dehradun town. Situated at the epicenter of SIDCUL IT Park, Doon Square is poised to be the hottest commercial destination in Dehradun's economic future. Spread over 4.2 acres, this 5 - storey project accommodates mall, multiplex, retail, serviced suites and hotel. The project also offers great returns on investment. Doon Square is located in the heart of state-of-the-art SIDCUL Information Park in more than 60 acres at Sahastradhara road, Dehradun, the state capital with intelligent buildings and hi-tech habitat centre. The state government has undertaken major initiatives in IT Education, formulation of a Pro-active IT Policy and development of International Quality infrastructure.

Opening Hours :- 10:00 AM - 07:00 PM


(Source)

There are many smalls food store in front of City junction mall . 2nd Floor, The City Junction Mall, Haridwar Bye Pass Road, Dehradun City, Dehradun - 248001, Near ISBT
